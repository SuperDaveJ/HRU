Federal Wage System (FWS)

The FWS pay schedule includes 130 appropriated fund and 118 non-appropriated fund local wage areas. Under the FWS, pay is based on what private industry is paying for comparable levels of work in the applicable local wage area. For each wage area, OPM identifies a lead agency, which is responsible for conducting wage surveys, analyzing data, and issuing wage schedules under the policies and procedures prescribed by OPM. Under this uniform pay system, an FWS employee’s pay will be:

  • The same as the pay of other federal jobs like his or hers in that wage area
  • In line with pay for private sector jobs like his or hers in that wage area
