Types of Services in the Federal Government

HR laws and regulations that have been discussed throughout this course also establish the types of federal civil services (i.e., categories of jobs) that are available in the federal government. The types of civil service positions are the:

  • Competitive service
  • Excepted service
  • Senior Executive Service
