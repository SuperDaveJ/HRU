Knowledge Check

There are four competences measured by OPMâ€™s FCAT-M: performance coaching and feedback, facilitating performance, differentiating performance, and building performance culture. Describe how you, as an HR professional, can be a part of developing these competencies in supervisors and managers.

 

Expert:

As an HR professional, I serve a key role in the Performance Coaching and Feedback competency by providing resources to managers and supervisors to help them improve those skills. In relation to the Facilitating Performance competency, I can advise managers and supervisors in identifying solutions for removing performance obstacles and in using appropriate methods for ensuring employee accountability and managing consequences.

I can assist supervisors in developing performance plans and in understanding how to measure results objectively, which helps them in Differentiating Performance. As a consultant to managers, I can guide them to resources for increasing their competency and skills in the area of Building Performance Culture.
