Hiring Authorities

Agencies may use a variety of hiring authorities (sometimes referred to as appointing authorities or hiring flexibilities) to hire job applicants into the competitive and excepted service. The more common types of hiring authorities available are:

  • Competitive Examining Authority
  • Merit Promotion
  • Noncompetitive Appointing Authorities
  • Excepted Appointment Authorities (which allow for conversion to the competitive service)
  • Excepted Service Appointments
  • Direct Hire Authority

Note: More information on the use of specific hiring authorities is covered throughout the courses in the OPM Staffing Specialist curriculum.
