Key Step #1: Identify Job and Assessment Tools

Tasks in this step include:

  • Conduct recruitment consultation (Review hiring authorities/flexibilities)
  • Identify associated Position Description (PD)
  • Conduct/review Job Analysis to identify competencies/KSAs
  • Identify assessment tools to use (e.g., crediting plans)

During this step, selecting assessment tools that evaluate applicants on the basis of job-related qualifications and competencies (rather than on the basis of age, race, or other potentially discriminating factors) is one way that you are implementing HR laws and regulations.
