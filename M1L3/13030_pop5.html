Key Step #5: Certify Eligibles

Tasks in this step include:

  • Place eligible candidates in order of selection
  • Send list of eligible candidates to manager/selecting official
  • Notify applicants
  • Interview and make selection
  • Audit by HR office (verification)
  • Notify applicants

Ensuring that Hiring Managers make legal selections in accordance with veterans’ preference and merit system principles is an important part of the HR Specialist’s job.
