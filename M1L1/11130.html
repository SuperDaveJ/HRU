Lesson Summary

Congratulations, you have completed Lesson 1: The Role of HR Professionals in the Federal Workplace.

In this lesson, you were introduced to the roles and responsibilities of HR professionals within the federal government. Now that you have completed this lesson, you should be able to:

  • Describe the role and value of HR professionals in the federal workplace
  • Describe the historical evolution of the federal workplace
  • Identify the phases of the HR Life Cycle
  • Identify the federal HR functions

In Lesson 2, you’ll learn about the legal basis for federal HR operations.
