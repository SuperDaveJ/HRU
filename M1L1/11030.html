Lesson Overview

This lesson, the Role of HR Professionals in the Federal Workplace, will introduce you to the roles and functions of HR professionals in the federal government.

In this lesson, you will learn to:

  • Describe the role and value of HR professionals in the federal workplace
  • Describe the historical evolution of the federal workplace
  • Identify the phases of the HR Life Cycle
  • Identify the federal HR functions (HR disciplines)

This lesson will take approximately 20 minutes to complete.
