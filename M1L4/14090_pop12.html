HR Specialist (Staffing)

A Human Resources Specialist (Staffing) is required to have knowledge of laws, Executive Orders, regulations, policies, concepts, and various HR policies, practices, procedures, and management controls of the staffing functions at a regionalized operations center. Staffing Specialists are required to have knowledge of HR staffing and recruitment principles, concepts, laws, regulations, techniques, and guidelines. Staffing professionals recruit, examine, select, and place employees.
