HR Specialist (Information Systems)

A Human Resources Specialist (Information Systems) is required to have knowledge of a wide variety of HR concepts, principles, laws, regulations, and practices and the interrelationships of various HR specialties as they relate to system processes/functions/capabilities and the infrastructure supporting the HR information system(s); and information technology concepts, practices, and procedures. Information Systems Specialists are required to have knowledge of local HR information system(s) and the HR transaction system. Information Systems professionals develop, deliver, manage, and maintain HR information systems using human resources management knowledge, rather than information technology.
