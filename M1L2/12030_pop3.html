Code of Federal Regulations (CFR)

The CFR is an arrangement of the rules published in the Federal Register by the executive departments and agencies of the federal government. The CFR contains regulations directing how to implement the laws in the U.S.C. It provides the official and complete text of agency regulations in a single publication. The CFR is arranged in a hierarchy of Titles, Volumes, Chapters, and Parts.

You can access the CFR at https://www.archives.gov/federal-register/cfr
