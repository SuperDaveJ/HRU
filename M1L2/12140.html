Knowledge Check

Now that you know the process of creating laws and regulations, how do you think this process affects your role as an HR professional?

 

Expert:

When the draft regulation is published, you can begin to see what changes will result. The public response to the proposed rule can give you an indication of the type of inquiries or concerns from your customers that you will need to address.

Once the final regulation is published, you will be expected to apply those changes to how you conduct your business as an HR professional.
