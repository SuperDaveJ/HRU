5 CFR Chapter I, Subchapter B

The majority of the regulations that are most relevant to HR professionals are found in 5 CFR Chapter I, Subchapter B. Review a description of selected 5 CFR parts by selecting each part from the list below.

This is only a selection of applicable regulations. Depending on your role, you may find that other parts are more relevant to your day-to-day activities. You can always visit www.ecfr.gov and select “Title 5 – Administrative Personnel” from the drop-down menu to learn more about these and other 5 CFR parts in more detail.
